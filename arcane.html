About show

The fantasy series Arcane is based on the popular computer game League of Legends and takes the viewer to a utopian world where class inequality has divided people into two cities. The upper city of Piltover is a bastion of progress and prosperity. Here luxury reigns and only the wealthy aristocracy lives, while the rest of the people live in the lower city of Zaun, where crime and poverty thrive. In the center of the plot are the sisters Vai and Powder from the lower city. After the death of the parents, the girls were raised by the guardian Vander, but he also failed to save the sisters from big problems when they committed a robbery with friends in the upper city, which marked the beginning of a serious armed conflict between Piltover and Zaun.
