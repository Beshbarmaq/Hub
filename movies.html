LUCY

Yesterday she was just a sexy blonde, and today she is the most dangerous and deadly creature on the planet with supernatural powers and intelligence. What quite recently the best minds of the world considered a fantastic theory has become a reality for her. And now from prey she will turn into a hunter.

KING KONG

In the 1930s, a film crew led by failed filmmaker Carl Denham travels to the mysterious Skull Island off Sumatra to study the legends of the giant gorilla Kong. Upon arrival, they discover that King Kong really exists. The gorilla lives in the most dense jungle.

JOHN WICK 3

Elite hitman John Wick for the third time an imperfect mistake. He violated the Continental assassin base, carrying out annihilation on its territory, and himself became a target for hordes of mercenaries. John has one last chance to escape New York.

INTERSTELLAR

Our time on Earth has come to an end, a team of explorers is taking on the most important mission in human history; traveling beyond our galaxy to see if humanity has a future among the stars.

TITANIC

Young lovers Jack and Rose find each other on the first and last voyage of the "unsinkable" Titanic. They could not know that a luxury liner would collide with an iceberg in the cold waters of the North Atlantic, and their passionate love would turn into a fight.

DEADPOOL

Wade Wilson is a mercenary. As a by-product of the military's "Weapon X" program, Wilson gained incredible strength, agility, and healing abilities. But at a terrible cost: his cellular structure is constantly changing, and his sanity is questionable. All Wilson wants is to stay afloat in the social cesspool.

CRUELLA

London in the 70s is embraced by the emerging culture of punk rock. An incredibly gifted swindler named Estella decides to make a name for herself in the fashion world. Her best friends are a couple of young pickpockets who appreciate Estella's passion for adventure and hope to win their place in the sun.

GOD FATHER

A crime saga about the New York-based Sicilian mafia family Corleone. The film covers the period 1945-1955. The novel poses questions about the origins and legitimacy of power by interrogating the notion that, as Balzac’s epigraph puts it, "Behind every great fortune there is a crime".

THE MAN WHO KNEW INFINITY

British biographical drama based on the novel of the same name by Robert Kanigel. The film tells about the short life of Indian self-taught mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan. It had its world premiere at the 2015 Toronto International Film Festival.
