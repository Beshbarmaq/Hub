What is the synopsis of the upcoming film?

The action takes place a year and a half after reporter Eddie Brock (Tom Hardy) was possessed by Venom, an intelligent alien symbiote. Thanks to him, Brock has superpowers, but also problems: Venom's hobby is to bite people's heads off. In a year and a half, Brock and Venom have learned to get along with each other. They cook breakfast together, go shopping, Brock forbids the Military to eat people, and he generally listens to the carrier. The idyll is disrupted by the evil symbiote Carnage, who inhabits the body of serial killer Cletus Cassidy (Woody Harrelson) and causes riots in the city. Only Brock and Venom can stop him..


The film included the filming of "Matrix 4"

The movie comic and the revival of the Lana Wachowski franchise were filmed in parallel in San Francisco. In the scene where Eddie Brock climbs to the top of the Coit Tower, helicopters are circling in the background, supposedly looking for Venom. In fact, these are helicopters from the filming of The Matrix: Resurrection, which took place in the city center.
